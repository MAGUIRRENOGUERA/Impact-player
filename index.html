OUR STORY

Where this
comes from.

Envolta Energía Global — Barcelona-based engineering and decarbonisation consultancy. Outside the office, we are mountain runners, cyclists, skiers, climbers.

The gap

Carbon accounting exists for organisations and products — but not for individuals. Athletes generate outsized footprints with no tools to manage them. We built one.

Why sport

Sport depends on nature. No snow, no skiing. No ocean, no surfing. Decarbonising sport is part of decarbonising all of ours.

"In sport, the same shift is overdue for environmental performance."

THE NUMBER
15–40 tCO₂e / year

The annual carbon footprint of a professional athlete

The European average is 6–8 tCO₂e per year. A single long-haul business class flight equals three months of an average person's emissions.
